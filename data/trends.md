Humanoid robots dominate conversation, with heavy skepticism about overhyped demos, remote-controlled "autonomous" systems, and Russia's collapsed Aidol debut generating mockery alongside genuine debate about whether humanoid form factors even make sense. Ukrainian defense robotics funding and Trinity Robotics' production scale-up reflects ongoing interest in wartime ground autonomy. Swarm robotics is a quieter but present thread, with researchers actively trying to build community around it.
